COMMISSION MISC.
Shinsoku Ikkon is an idol unit in THE iDOLM@STER: SideM. It consists of the firey Suzaku Akai and the cool-headed Genbu Kurono. Both members are delinquents who attend the same school. Joined by Suzaku's trusty little cat Nyako, the duo perform action packed songs to show off their BURNIN' spirit and stop bad guys!

Shinsoku are my favorite iDOLM@STER idols overall, and I would like others to be able to understand them better and find information about them easily. So, here's a guide on all of Shinsoku's translated content!

TRANSLATION
This is all of the translated content featuring Shinsoku as major characters, excluding LIVE ON STAGE! content. All are in chronological order. Important stories that I think are must-reads are marked with ★.
  1. Signlight Illumination Event: When the lights go out during a concert, Suzaku uses his creativity to fix the problem.
  2. Office One-Shots (2014): Stories of daily life with Mofumofuen.
  3. Office One-Shots (2015): Stories of daily life with Mofumofuen (again).
  4. Office One-Shots (2015): Stories of daily life with Cafe Parade.
  5. Art Museum Live: Suzaku learns to express himself through art.
  6. SL Drama CD: Shinsoku prepare to work with a kindergarten for the day. ★
  7. SL Drama CD (Cafe Parade): Shinsoku talk to Cafe Parade for the first time, and Nyako runs off with Satan.
  8. Traditional Japanese Crafts: Suzaku deals with his anxiety issues around hosting a live TV segment for the first time.
  9. Office One-Shots (2016): Stories of daily life with S.E.M.
  10. Job Challenge (TBA): Shinsoku Ikkon try out new jobs and Genbu meets someone from his distant past who also knows Hideo. ★★★
  11. Three Kingdoms: When exploring Chinatown in preparation for their new film, Genbu meets the man who kicked him out of his hometown. ★★★
  12. Aside Famille: Shinsoku deal with the consequences of the events of the Chinatown incident. ★★★
  13. Pirate's Treasure: The pair hang out at a pirate themed amusement as reference for their next film.
  14. 4th Anniversary Live: Along with THE Kogado, Shinsoku reminisce over their days at 315 Productions. ★
  15. Night & Day: Shinsoku go on a ski trip with Altessimo and W.
  16. Crimson Live: Shinsoku show Jupiter around their school to demonstrate what a delinquent's school is like. ★
  17. NSE Drama CD (Beit): Shinsoku learn how to use makeup from Beit.
