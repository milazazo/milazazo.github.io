INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
By default, all products will be sent through letter mail at the following rates:
  • Under 80g: $4.50 NZD (untracked)
  • Between 80g~ 200g: $7.00 NZD (untracked)
  • Over 200g: $17.50 NZD (sent as parcel, tracked)
Product weight info is below.
DOMESTIC SHIPPING
Orders within New Zealand are shipped at the following rates:
  • Under 500g: $2.00 NZD
  • Over 500g: $3.00 NZD
PRODUCT WEIGHT
Product weights are approximate and include the weight of any protective material such as cardboard.
  • Doujinshi + Sticker + Print (Shinsoku): 90g
  • Sticker (pack of 4): 12g
