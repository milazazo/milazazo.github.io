MAIN SITE
Welcome to milaza/iM@S!

This section of the site is dedicated to my favorite video game series, THE iDOLM@STER. I love all iDOLM@STER branches, especially SideM and Shiny Colors. I've been a GenbuP and ShinsokuP since 22nd July, 2016, and an AmanaP since 2019.
CurrentlyI've played Cinderella Girls (Mobage), Deresute, Million Live! (Gree) and Mirishita as well!
, I play SideM (Mobage), Shiny Colors, and Pop Links. Looking forward to playing GROWING STARS in the future!

My love of iM@S began in 2016, when I first met Genbu. He's been my favorite character ever since! I must've drawn hundreds of images of him across the years. Aside from fanart, I also post(ed) translations and collect merch (and yet, still no one recognizes my itabag). Besides Genbu and Amana, I also adore Jupiter, Amehiko, Natsuki (both CG/M), Syuko, Ryo (CG), and Sakuya. For more detail on my personal feelings for iM@S, click here!
