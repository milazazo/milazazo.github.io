ART RESOURCES A list of art-related bookmarks I find interesting enough to keep around. Most are 3D/game development related. Not necessarily resources/tutorials that can help you directly.

Animation:
  • The Animation of Guilty Gear Xrd & Dragon Ball FighterZ : A look into how Guilty Gear achieves its unique 3D cel shaded style.

Modelling:
  • Guide to that elusive “PS1-pixelated-lowpoly”(but not really): Overview of low poly art in old games and how it was used.
  • Low-Poly Character Modeling and Texturing: Comphrehensive walkthrough of the creation of a low-poly yet realistic character in 3DSMax.
  • Travelling Cat: A walkthrough of the creation of a hand painted character model.
  • Giant's End Village: Making an Environment in the Ghibli Style: A walkthrough of the creation of a painterly environment.

Illustration:
  • Analyzing Capcom’s Anatomy Guide with English Translation: Translation of Capcom's guide to anatomy for its fighting games.
