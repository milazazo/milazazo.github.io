THE FANTASTIC FIREMAN!
Creator: milazaYear: 2021
Page/size: 40/A5Price: 12.5 (USD)
Release: Overload 2022Content: Comic
Why the hell did you guys let me write something as stupid as this? That's what I want to know. Anyway, this is the cringe "ultimate Shinsoku" dojinshi I worked on for like three years (I took a year off in the middle to read the Bible though). The story is really bad, don't read it. But please buy a copy anyway since I have some sitting left at home. The art is still pretty good, I think... for the first six pages.

I was super into superheroes when I started writing it, so the plot is about Shinsoku performing as heroes/villains in a play and the meta of fiction affecting reality or some dumb crap like that (this has nothing to do with shipping. Get away from me). I also forced a lot of other iM@S characters into the plot because I love cross-branch. Overall, I would never like to write another SideM dojinshi again!
